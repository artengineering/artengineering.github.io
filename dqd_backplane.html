BACKPLANE FOR DQD SYSTEM

Designed to work as a part of the modular system for protecting superconducting magnets (Digital Quench Detection System). Backplane connects the modules and to the controller.

What I have done:

  • Generated idea
  • Designed complete schematic in Altium
  • Chose components for the board
  • Ordered components
  • Did the layout of the PCB in Altium
  • Ordered the PCB and PCB assembly
  • Troubleshooted and tested the board

PCB features and characteristics:

  • Mechanical: width - 19", height - 6U
  • 8 slots for the modules and 1 slot for the controller
  • Protection against reversed voltage polarity
